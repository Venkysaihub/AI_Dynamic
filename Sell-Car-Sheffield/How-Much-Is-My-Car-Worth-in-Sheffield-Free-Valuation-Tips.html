What Factors Affect Your Car’s Value?

Several elements influence the value of your car in Sheffield. Understanding these factors will help you set realistic expectations and maximise your return.

  • Mileage – Lower mileage often means a higher price.
  • Service history – A full service record adds value and trust.
  • Condition – Interior and exterior wear and tear impact price.
  • Market demand – Prices fluctuate based on buyer interest in your area.
