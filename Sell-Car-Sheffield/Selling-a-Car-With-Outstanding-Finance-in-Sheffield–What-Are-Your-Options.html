Selling a Car with Outstanding Finance in Sheffield – What Are Your Options?

If you’re considering selling your car in Sheffield but still have outstanding finance on it, you may be unsure of what to do. At Sell Car Sheffield, we understand how to navigate the complexities of selling a car with finance left to pay. Here are your options for a smooth sale.

1. Pay Off the Outstanding Finance

The most straightforward option is to pay off the remaining finance balance before selling. Once the finance is settled, you can sell the car freely. If you choose this route, you’ll be able to keep any proceeds from the sale. However, at Sell Car Sheffield, we can help you sell your car without having to clear the finance yourself by paying off the balance directly.

2. Sell Your Car to a Dealer or Buyer With Finance Outstanding

Another option is to sell your car to a trusted dealer or buyer, like Sell Car Sheffield. We can help settle the remaining finance as part of the sale process. We’ll pay off your finance directly and transfer the car to the buyer, leaving you with the difference, if any. This is a great option if you want a hassle-free, fast sale.

3. Sell the Car for Less Than What You Owe

If the sale price of the car doesn’t cover the full amount of the finance, you will need to pay the difference to clear the debt. This is known as negative equity. Sell Car Sheffield can help you understand if this is the best route for you and offer a fair valuation that considers your outstanding finance.

4. Part-Exchange Your Car for Another

If you're planning to purchase another car, a part-exchange is an option. Dealers may offer to pay off your outstanding finance as part of the deal. At Sell Car Sheffield, we can provide a competitive valuation for your car, even if it has outstanding finance, and ensure that the finance is cleared during the transaction.

5. Voluntary Termination of Your Finance Agreement

If you're struggling with your car finance payments, you may have the option to voluntarily terminate the agreement under the Consumer Credit Act. This allows you to return the car to the finance company without further payments. After returning the car, you can sell it. However, this can impact your credit score. Sell Car Sheffield can help you assess your options if you're considering this route.

6. Check for Any Early Settlement Fees

Before selling your car, it’s important to check if there are any early settlement fees associated with paying off your finance. These fees could reduce the profit from the sale. When you sell to Sell Car Sheffield, we’ll work with you to ensure that your finance is cleared as smoothly as possible and that you're fully aware of any costs involved.
