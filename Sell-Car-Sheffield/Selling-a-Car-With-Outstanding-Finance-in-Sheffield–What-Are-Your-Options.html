3. Negative Equity – What to Do If You Owe More Than the Car's Value

If your car is worth less than the remaining finance, you may find yourself in a position of negative equity. In this case, you would need to pay the difference between the sale price and the remaining finance. Sell Car Sheffield can provide you with a fair valuation and help you determine whether selling is the best option for you, even if you owe more than the car is worth.

4. Consider Part-Exchange Options

If you plan to buy another car, part-exchange might be an option for you. Dealers like Sell Car Sheffield offer part-exchange services, where we pay off your finance and apply the remaining value of your current car towards your next purchase. This is a quick and efficient way to manage both the sale and your next vehicle.

5. Voluntary Termination of Your Finance Agreement

If you're struggling to make payments, you may be able to terminate your finance agreement early by returning the car to the finance company. This is known as voluntary termination. While this can help reduce your debt, it’s important to note that it might affect your credit score. Sell Car Sheffield can guide you through this process if you're considering voluntary termination.

6. Check for Early Settlement Fees

Before selling your car, make sure to check if there are any early settlement fees with your finance provider. These fees can impact the overall amount you receive from the sale.
