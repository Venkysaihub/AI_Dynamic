Electric Vehicle Recovery: Specialized Solutions for EV Owners

Electric vehicles (EVs) offer many advantages, including environmental benefits, fuel savings, and reduced maintenance. However, EVs also present unique challenges during recovery, particularly with their sensitive battery and electric drivetrain. Proper handling during breakdowns, accidents, or emergency recoveries is crucial to avoid damage to these components.

The Importance of Protecting the EV Battery During Recovery

The high-voltage lithium-ion battery in an EV is sensitive and expensive. If damaged during recovery, it can lead to costly repairs or safety hazards.

  • Risks of Battery Damage: Improper towing could damage the battery, rendering the vehicle unsafe or requiring expensive repairs.
  • Fire Hazards: A damaged battery can lead to thermal runaway, causing fires.

Car Recovery Liverpool uses flatbed towing, the safest method to avoid battery and drivetrain damage.

Flatbed Towing: The Best Option for EV Recovery

Unlike traditional vehicles, EVs should not be towed with wheels on the ground, which can damage the drivetrain. Flatbed towing ensures the vehicle is fully lifted, protecting its sensitive components.

  • Protects the Battery and Motor: Flatbed towing prevents strain on the wheels, drivetrain, and battery.
  • Prevents Further Damage: Flatbed towing ensures the EV remains neutral, safeguarding the battery and motor.

Car Recovery Liverpool uses flatbed trucks designed specifically for EV recovery.
