How to Prepare Your Vehicle for Scrap Removal

Before you scrap your old car, it’s essential to prepare it properly. Here’s a guide to ensure a smooth process.

1. Remove All Personal Belongings

Thoroughly check your car for personal items, especially in compartments like under seats, the glove box, and the trunk.

2. Gather Your Ownership Documents

Prepare documents like the title of ownership, vehicle registration, and proof of identity.

3. Take Out Valuable Parts

Remove valuable components like the catalytic converter, tires, and batteries, especially if they’re still in good condition.

4. Take the License Plates Off

Remove the license plates and return them to the DMV or keep them for your next vehicle.

5. Remove the Car Battery

Disconnect the battery to avoid electrical issues, especially if the car is old. If unsure, Car Recovery Liverpool can assist with this.

6. Review Scrap Laws and Regulations

Ensure you understand the legal requirements for scrap car disposal, including ownership transfer and scrap certification.
