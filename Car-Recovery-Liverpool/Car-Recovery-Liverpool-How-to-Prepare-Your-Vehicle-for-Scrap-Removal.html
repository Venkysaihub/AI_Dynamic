How to Prepare Your Vehicle for Scrap Removal

Preparing your vehicle for scrap removal is a straightforward process if you follow these essential steps.

1. Clean Out Personal Belongings

Check areas like under the seats, the trunk, and the glove compartment for any remaining personal items.

2. Gather Vehicle Ownership Documentation

Ensure you have the title of ownership, vehicle registration, and proof of identity ready for the scrap process.

3. Disconnect the Battery and Remove Fluids

Disconnect the battery to avoid electrical hazards and drain fluids like oil and coolant to prevent environmental damage.

4. Consider Removing Recyclable Components

Valuable parts like the catalytic converter, copper wiring, radiators, and tires can be removed for recycling and added value.
