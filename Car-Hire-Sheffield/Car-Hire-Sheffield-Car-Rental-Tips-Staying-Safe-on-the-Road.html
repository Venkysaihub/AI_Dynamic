2. Check the Vehicle Before Driving

Before starting your journey, inspect your rental car to ensure everything is in order.

What to Check:

  • Look for any existing scratches, dents, or damages and report them to the rental company.
  • Check the fuel level and understand the refueling policy.
  • Test headlights, indicators, and brakes to ensure they are functioning properly.
  • Familiarize yourself with controls like wipers, hazard lights, and air conditioning.

3. Follow Sheffield’s Driving Rules

Whether you're a local or visiting Sheffield, it's important to adhere to traffic regulations for a safe and hassle-free driving experience.

Key Rules to Remember:

  • Observe speed limits, especially in residential areas and near schools.
  • Avoid driving in bus lanes unless signage permits rental vehicles.
  • Use hands-free devices if you need to make phone calls while driving.
  • Be mindful of parking restrictions and always check signage before leaving your vehicle.

By following these rules, you can avoid fines and ensure a smooth journey with your rental car.

5. Return the Vehicle on Time

To avoid late fees and ensure smooth service for the next customer, return your rental car on the agreed date and time.
