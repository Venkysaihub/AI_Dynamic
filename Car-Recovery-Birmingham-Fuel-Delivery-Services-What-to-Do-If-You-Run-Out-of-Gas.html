1. Prevention is Better Than Cure

Avoid the situation by keeping an eye on your fuel level and refueling early.

  • Refuel Regularly: Refuel at regular intervals to prevent running low.
  • Keep a Fuel Reserve: Carry a small reserve of fuel for emergencies, especially on long trips.
  • Track Your Fuel Usage: Many vehicles have fuel trackers that help you monitor when to refuel.

Despite planning, running out of fuel can still happen. Here’s how to deal with it.

2. What to Do If You Run Out of Fuel

Stay calm and follow these steps to get help.

  • Pull Over to Safety: Move to a safe location.
  • Activate Hazard Lights: Ensure visibility to other drivers.
  • Assess the Situation: Estimate the distance to a fuel station or a safe spot to wait.
