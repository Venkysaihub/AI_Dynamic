What Is NLP and Why Does It Matter for Content Optimization?

NLP is a subfield of artificial intelligence that enables machines to understand, interpret, and generate human language. In content marketing, NLP analyzes user intent, keyword relevance, tone, structure, and semantic meaning—allowing content to align more effectively with search engine algorithms and reader expectations.
