Why Flatbed Towing is the Best Method for EV Recovery

For safe EV recovery, flatbed towing is the recommended method. Unlike conventional towing, flatbed towing lifts the entire vehicle off the ground, ensuring that no strain is placed on the vehicle’s drivetrain or battery.

  • Prevents Damage: Flatbed towing completely lifts the vehicle, avoiding any potential damage to the drivetrain or battery.
  • Maintains Proper Alignment: Flatbed towing ensures that the EV remains in a neutral position throughout the entire recovery process.
