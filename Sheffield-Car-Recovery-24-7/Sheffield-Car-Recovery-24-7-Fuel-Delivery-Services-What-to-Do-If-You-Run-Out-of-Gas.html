2. What to Do When You Run Out of Fuel in Sheffield

If you find yourself stuck without fuel in Sheffield, follow these essential steps to stay safe:

  • Pull Over to a Safe Spot: Move your vehicle off the road to a safe location, away from traffic.
  • Activate Hazard Lights: Turn on your hazard lights to alert other drivers, especially in low visibility or during nighttime.
  • Assess Your Location: Check your surroundings and estimate the distance to the nearest gas station or safe area in Sheffield.
