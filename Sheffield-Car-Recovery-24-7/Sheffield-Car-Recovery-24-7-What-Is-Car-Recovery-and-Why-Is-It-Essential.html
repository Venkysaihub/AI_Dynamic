What Is Car Recovery?

Car recovery involves transporting vehicles that cannot be driven due to breakdowns, accidents, or other issues. Common scenarios include:

  • Breakdowns: Engine failures, overheating, or electrical problems.
  • Accidents: Safe removal of damaged vehicles from crash sites.
  • Flat Tires or Dead Batteries: When immediate repairs aren’t possible.
  • Specialized Recovery: For electric cars, luxury vehicles, or unique needs.
