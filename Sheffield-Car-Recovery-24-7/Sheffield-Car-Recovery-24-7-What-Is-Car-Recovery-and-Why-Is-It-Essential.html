What Is Car Recovery?

Car recovery involves the professional retrieval and transportation of vehicles that are no longer drivable. This service is crucial in situations like breakdowns, accidents, or roadside emergencies. Common scenarios include:

  • Breakdowns: Engine failures, dead batteries, or transmission issues.
  • Accidents: Safe removal of damaged vehicles from crash sites.
  • Flat Tires or Fuel Shortages: When immediate repairs aren’t possible.
  • Specialized Recovery: For electric vehicles, luxury cars, or unique needs.
