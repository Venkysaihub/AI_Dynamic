The Risks of DIY Phone Repairs

DIY phone repairs can seem cost-effective, but they come with several risks:

  • Potential for Further Damage: Improper handling or use of low-quality replacement parts can cause even more damage to your phone.
  • Void Your Warranty: Many phone manufacturers void warranties if you attempt repairs yourself, which could be costly if further issues arise.
  • Lack of Proper Tools: DIY kits often come with subpar tools that can scratch or damage your phone, leading to an imperfect repair.
  • Complicated Process: Replacing a screen involves delicate steps, and even a small mistake can render your phone unusable.
