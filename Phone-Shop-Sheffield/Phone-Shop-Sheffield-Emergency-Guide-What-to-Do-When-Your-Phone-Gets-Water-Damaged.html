Emergency Guide: What to Do When Your Phone Gets Water Damaged

Accidentally dropped your phone in water? Whether it's a sink, toilet, or puddle, water damage can be a nightmare for any phone user. The good news is that acting quickly can increase the chances of saving your device. In this guide, we'll walk you through the immediate steps to take and how Phone Shop Sheffield can help with expert water damage repairs.

Step 1: Turn Off Your Phone Immediately

Powering down your phone as soon as possible prevents short circuits. Avoid pressing any buttons or trying to test if it still works—this can make things worse.

Step 2: Remove Any External Components

Take out the SIM card, memory card, and any accessories. If your phone has a removable battery, take that out as well to minimize further damage.

Step 3: Dry the Exterior

Use a dry, lint-free cloth or paper towel to gently blot away excess water. Avoid shaking the phone or using a hairdryer, as heat can push moisture further inside.

Step 4: Avoid Common Myths

Many people believe putting a wet phone in rice will absorb the moisture, but this is not an effective solution. Rice particles can enter your phone and cause additional issues. Instead, let the device air-dry in a cool, dry place.

Step 5: Seek Professional Repair Services

If your phone has been exposed to water, internal components may corrode over time, even if it seems to be working fine now. The best way to ensure your phone is safe is by getting it checked by professionals.
