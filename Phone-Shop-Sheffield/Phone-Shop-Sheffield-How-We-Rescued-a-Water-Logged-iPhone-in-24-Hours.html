The Situation: An iPhone That Fell Into Water

A customer walked into Phone Shop Sheffield in a state of panic. Their iPhone had accidentally fallen into a pool, and they feared it was completely ruined. With water damage being a major concern, they turned to us for help.

Step 1: Initial Inspection and Diagnosis

Our first step was to perform a thorough inspection. We carefully opened the device to check for signs of water inside the phone and to assess the damage. The key to saving a water-damaged phone is quick action, so we immediately began drying and cleaning the components.

  • Checked for visible water damage and corrosion inside the device.
  • Examined internal parts like the battery, motherboard, and camera for water exposure.

Step 2: Drying and Cleaning

After confirming water damage, we began the drying process. Using specialized tools, we carefully dried out the internal components to prevent further damage. Water that stays in the device can lead to corrosion and permanent damage, so this step is critical in the repair process.

  • Used professional drying techniques and tools to remove moisture from all internal parts.
  • Cleaned components to prevent corrosion and ensure the device would function properly once reassembled.

Step 3: Testing and Repairing

Once we were confident that the device was dry, we tested the phone to ensure everything was working as it should. We checked the screen, battery, camera, and other functions, making sure that there were no issues with any components. If any parts were damaged, we replaced them to ensure the phone was fully functional.

  • Tested the phone’s functionality, including the touch screen, sound, and camera.
  • Replaced damaged parts, such as the battery, charging port, or display, to restore full function.

The Result: A Fully Repaired iPhone in Just 24 Hours

After completing the repair, the iPhone was tested again and was found to be in perfect working condition. The customer was overjoyed to have their phone back in only 24 hours, just as good as new.
