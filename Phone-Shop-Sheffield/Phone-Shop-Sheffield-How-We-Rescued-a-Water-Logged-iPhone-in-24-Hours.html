Case Study: How We Rescued a Water-Logged iPhone in 24 Hours

When an iPhone accidentally gets submerged in water, it can be a stressful experience. Fortunately, at Phone Shop Sheffield, we specialize in water damage repairs, offering expert solutions that can bring your device back to life. The Problem: A Water-Logged iPhone

One of our customers came into Phone Shop Sheffield in a panic after accidentally dropping their iPhone into a pool. With the device fully submerged for several minutes, the customer feared that the phone was beyond repair. However, we knew that acting quickly and efficiently could help save the device.

Step 1: Initial Assessment

The first thing our technicians did was to assess the extent of the water damage. We carefully dried off the phone and opened it up to inspect the internal components. Water damage can cause corrosion, so we checked for any visible signs of damage or residue.

  • Examined the internal components for water residue or corrosion.
  • Checked the phone’s water damage indicators to determine the level of exposure.

Step 2: Cleaning and Drying

Once we confirmed the water damage, we took immediate action to clean and dry the internal components. This step is crucial because it helps prevent further damage and corrosion. Using specialized tools and techniques, we meticulously dried out the device and cleaned the affected areas.

  • Used professional-grade tools to dry and clean the device’s internal parts.
  • Ensured that no water remained in hard-to-reach areas like the charging port or headphone jack.

Step 3: Testing and Repair

After thoroughly cleaning the iPhone, we began the testing phase. The goal was to ensure that all functions, including the display, camera, and speakers, were working as expected. If any issues were found, we would replace damaged parts to restore the phone to its full functionality.

  • Tested all features, including the touch screen, camera, and sound.
  • Replaced any components that were beyond repair, such as the battery or charging port.

The Result: A Fully Functioning iPhone in 24 Hours

Thanks to our expertise and quick action, we were able to return the fully repaired iPhone to the customer within just 24 hours. The phone was in perfect working order, with no signs of water damage. Our customer was thrilled to have their device back in such a short amount of time!
