2. When Is It Time to Upgrade?

While repairs are often a great option, there are times when upgrading your phone is the better choice. Here’s how you can tell if it’s time for an upgrade:

  • Frequent Breakdowns: If your phone is constantly malfunctioning despite repairs, it may be time for a new one.
  • Outdated Features: Older models may not support the latest software updates or features, which can limit your device’s performance.
  • Slow Performance: If your phone is significantly slower than it used to be and repairs don’t fix the problem, upgrading could provide a smoother experience.
