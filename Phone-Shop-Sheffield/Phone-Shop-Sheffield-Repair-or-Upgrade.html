2. When Is It Time to Upgrade?

While repairs are often a great option, there are times when upgrading your phone is the more practical solution. If your phone is old or experiencing frequent breakdowns, upgrading might be the better choice.

How to Tell if You Should Upgrade:

  • Frequent Failures: If your phone is constantly malfunctioning despite repairs, it may be time for a new device.
  • Outdated Features: Older models may not support the latest software or features, leaving you behind on new capabilities.
  • Slow Performance: If your phone is running sluggishly and repairs can’t fix the issue, it might be time to get an upgrade.
