Extend Your Phone’s Lifespan: 7 Easy Habits to Save Money

1. Use a Protective Case

One of the best ways to prevent damage from drops and impacts is by using a protective case. At Phone Shop Sheffield, we offer a variety of durable cases designed to safeguard your device.

  • Shockproof Cases: Ideal for those who need extra protection for their phone.

2. Clean Your Phone Regularly

Keeping your phone clean is essential not just for aesthetics but for functionality too. Dirt and debris can damage ports and the screen over time.

  • Microfiber Cloth: Use a soft microfiber cloth to clean the screen and body without leaving scratches.
  • Disinfecting: Clean your phone with disinfecting wipes to reduce the buildup of germs and dirt.

3. Avoid Overcharging Your Battery

Charging your phone to 100% regularly or letting it drop to 0% can shorten battery lifespan. Keep your battery between 20% and 80% for optimal performance.

At Phone Shop Sheffield, we offer high-quality chargers and portable power banks, so you can keep your phone charged without harming the battery.

4. Install a Screen Protector

Protect your phone's screen from scratches and cracks by installing a screen protector. At Phone Shop Sheffield, we offer a variety of options to suit different needs.

  • Tempered Glass: Stronger and more resistant to cracks and scratches.
  • Plastic Film: A thinner, more affordable option that still provides protection.

Using a screen protector will help you avoid costly screen repairs and keep your phone looking new for longer.

5. Update Your Software Regularly

Keeping your phone's software up to date ensures it runs efficiently and fixes any bugs that may slow down its performance.

6. Free Up Storage Space

Overloading your phone with apps, photos, and videos can cause it to slow down. Regularly clearing your storage or moving files to cloud storage helps maintain its performance.
