What Is Car Recovery?

Car recovery refers to the transportation of vehicles that cannot be driven due to various issues. Common scenarios include:

  • Breakdowns: Mechanical failures, engine overheating, or electrical issues.
  • Accidents: Safe removal of damaged vehicles from crash sites.
  • Flat Tires or Dead Batteries: When on-the-spot repairs are not possible.
  • Specialized Recovery: For electric cars, luxury vehicles, or other specific needs.
