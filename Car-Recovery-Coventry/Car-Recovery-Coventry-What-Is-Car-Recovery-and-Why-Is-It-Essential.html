What Is Car Recovery?

Car recovery refers to professional services that retrieve and transport vehicles from places where they are no longer drivable. This might involve towing your car to a garage, delivering it to your home, or even moving it over long distances.

Key recovery scenarios include:

  • Breakdowns: Whether it’s a faulty transmission or a dead battery, recovery services come to your rescue.
  • Post-Collision Recovery: Safely removing damaged vehicles from accident scenes.
  • Flat Tires or Fuel Shortages: Addressing common roadside emergencies that leave you stranded.
  • Repossession Services: Handling the retrieval of vehicles for legal or financial matters.
