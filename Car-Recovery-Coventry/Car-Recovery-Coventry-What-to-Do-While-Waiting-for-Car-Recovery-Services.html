1. Prioritize Safety and Visibility

Your safety is the most important thing when your car breaks down. Follow these steps to stay safe:

  • Move to a Safe Area: As soon as you notice something wrong, pull your car off the road to a safe area. If you can’t move it, stay away from the car and avoid high-traffic lanes.
  • Turn on Hazard Lights: Always activate your hazard lights to alert other drivers, especially in low-visibility conditions or at night.
  • Stay Inside Your Vehicle (If Safe): If you're on a busy road, stay inside your car with your seatbelt fastened and doors locked for added safety.
