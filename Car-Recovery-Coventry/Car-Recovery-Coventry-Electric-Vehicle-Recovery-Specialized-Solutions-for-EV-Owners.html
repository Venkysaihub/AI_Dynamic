Flatbed Towing for Safe EV Recovery

Flatbed towing is the recommended method for EV recovery. Unlike traditional towing, flatbed towing lifts the entire vehicle off the ground, protecting its components. This method is essential for preserving your EV’s drivetrain and battery.

  • Prevents Damage: Flatbed towing ensures the vehicle isn’t subjected to strain, safeguarding the battery and drivetrain.
  • Proper Alignment: This method ensures the EV remains in a neutral position during transport.
