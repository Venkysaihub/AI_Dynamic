The Importance of Protecting Your EV Battery During Recovery

The high-voltage lithium-ion batteries in electric vehicles are sensitive and expensive. If mishandled during recovery, they can suffer damage, leading to costly repairs and potential safety hazards.

  • Risk of Battery Damage: Improper towing can cause significant damage to the EV battery, which may result in expensive repairs or rendering the vehicle unsafe.
  • Fire Hazards: A damaged EV battery can cause thermal runaway, leading to dangerous fires.

At Car Recovery Sheffield 24/7, we prioritize safety and use flatbed towing – the safest method to protect your EV’s battery and drivetrain during recovery.

Flatbed Towing: The Optimal Solution for EV Recovery

Unlike traditional vehicles, EVs should never be towed with wheels on the ground, as this can cause damage to the drivetrain and battery. Flatbed towing completely lifts the vehicle off the ground, ensuring it’s transported safely without damaging sensitive components.

  • Protects the Battery & Motor: Flatbed towing eliminates strain on the wheels, battery, and drivetrain, ensuring safe transport.
  • Prevents Additional Damage: By using flatbed towing, we avoid causing further harm to your EV, keeping it in optimal condition.
