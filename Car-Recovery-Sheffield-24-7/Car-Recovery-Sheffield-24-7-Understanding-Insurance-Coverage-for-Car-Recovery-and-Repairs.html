What Is Car Recovery Insurance Coverage?

Car recovery coverage is a part of your insurance policy that covers the cost of towing or transporting your car after a breakdown or accident. This coverage can include roadside assistance, towing to a garage, and sometimes even repairs. Understanding your policy ensures you’re prepared for unexpected situations.

How to Check for Roadside Assistance:
