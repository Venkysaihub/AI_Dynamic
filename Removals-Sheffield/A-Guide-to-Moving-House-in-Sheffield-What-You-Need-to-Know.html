Packing Tips for a Safe Move

Notify Essential Services and Change Your Address

Remember to inform utility providers, banks, and the postal service about your move to avoid any service interruptions or missed communications.

Prepare a “First Night” Essentials Box

Pack a separate box with essential items such as toiletries, phone chargers, medications, and snacks to keep handy when you arrive at your new home.

Take Measurements and Plan Your Layout

Measure doorways and rooms in your new property to ensure furniture fits comfortably, avoiding last-minute surprises.
