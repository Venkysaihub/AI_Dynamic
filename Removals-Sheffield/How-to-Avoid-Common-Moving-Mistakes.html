3. Avoid Overloading Boxes

Heavy or improperly packed boxes can cause injuries or damage. Pack items by weight and use smaller boxes for heavier objects. Clearly label boxes to make unpacking easier.

4. Measure Furniture and Doorways

Measure your large furniture and doorways before moving day to ensure smooth access. This helps avoid delays and prevents damage during the move.

5. Declutter Before You Move

Moving is a great opportunity to donate, sell, or discard items you no longer need. Decluttering reduces moving costs and saves you time unpacking.

6. Keep Important Documents and Valuables Separate

Keep essentials like passports, contracts, and valuables with you rather than packing them with other items to avoid loss during transit.
