DIY Moving: When It Might Work

DIY moving can work if you’re relocating a small number of items, have a flexible schedule, and access to the right equipment and helpers. However, it’s essential to prepare thoroughly and understand the risks.

  • Cost-Saving: You may save on labour, but rental costs, fuel, and equipment add up.
  • Time-Consuming: Packing and moving often takes longer than expected.
  • Lack of Experience: Without training, damage and injuries are more likely.
