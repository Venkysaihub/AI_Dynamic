How to Prepare Your Vehicle for Scrap Removal

When it’s time to scrap your old, non-functional vehicle, it’s important to prepare for a smooth and efficient process. Below is a checklist to ensure everything is handled correctly before your car is picked up for scrap removal.

1. Remove Personal Items from the Car

Clear out all personal belongings from your car, such as papers, clothing, and trash.

2. Gather Your Vehicle’s Paperwork

Ensure you have essential documents like the vehicle registration, proof of identity, and title/deed.

3. Disconnect the Battery

To prevent electrical hazards and to properly dispose of the battery, disconnect the negative terminal. If unsure, a professional service like Car Recovery London can help.

4. Remove Recyclable Parts

Consider removing valuable parts like the catalytic converter, tires, and metal components to maximize scrap value. This may not always be necessary, as Car Recovery London handles recycling.

5. Remove License Plates

License plates must be returned to the DMV or kept for future use, depending on local regulations.

6. Check Local Scrap Laws and Regulations

Familiarize yourself with local laws regarding scrap vehicle disposal, including ownership transfer and vehicle registration.
