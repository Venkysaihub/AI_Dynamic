What Is Car Recovery?

Car recovery involves the safe removal and transportation of vehicles that cannot be driven. This might include:

  • Breakdowns: Sudden mechanical failures, engine overheating, or electrical issues.
  • Accidents: Safely recovering damaged vehicles from collision sites.
  • Repossession: Retrieving vehicles as part of financial or legal proceedings.
  • Flat Tires or Dead Batteries: When roadside assistance is not enough to resolve the issue.

Car recovery professionals utilize specialized equipment such as flatbeds, winches, and tow trucks to ensure vehicles are handled with care.
