What Affects How Long Car Recovery Takes?

While we aim to arrive as quickly as possible, here are the main elements that can impact arrival time:

  • Location: Central areas are reached quicker than remote or rural parts.
  • Traffic conditions: Peak hours can slow response due to congestion.
  • Weather: Adverse conditions like snow or fog can delay arrivals.
  • Type of breakdown: A battery jump-start is faster than recovering a damaged vehicle.
  • Vehicle size: Larger or commercial vehicles may need special equipment.
