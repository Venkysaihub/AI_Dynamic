  • Sensitivity to Damage: Towing an EV improperly may damage its battery, causing malfunction or even complete failure.
  • Safety Hazards: A compromised battery increases the risk of fires or electrical hazards.

Flatbed Towing: The Safest Method for EV Recovery

Flatbed towing is widely regarded as the safest and most effective method for recovering electric vehicles. This technique ensures that the entire vehicle is lifted off the ground, reducing the risk of damage to vital components like the battery and drivetrain.

  • Prevents Damage: Flatbed towing eliminates the risk of stress on your EV’s battery and drivetrain.
  • Neutral Position: The vehicle remains properly aligned during transport, avoiding potential damage.
