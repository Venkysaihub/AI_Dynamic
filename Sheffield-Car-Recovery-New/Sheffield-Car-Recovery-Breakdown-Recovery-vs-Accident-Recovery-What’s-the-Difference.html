What Is Breakdown Recovery?

Breakdown recovery refers to assistance when your car stops working due to a mechanical or electrical issue. Common causes include:

  • Flat battery
  • Engine failure
  • Punctured or flat tyre
  • Running out of fuel
  • Overheating

In many cases, our technicians can resolve minor issues on-site. If not, we’ll tow your vehicle to a garage or destination of your choice in Sheffield.

What Is Accident Recovery?

Accident recovery is required when your vehicle is involved in a road traffic collision or has suffered physical damage that makes it unsafe or impossible to drive. This includes:

  • Severe crashes or collisions
  • Cars stuck off-road or in ditches
  • Airbag deployment or frame damage

We recover vehicles safely and support you through the insurance process if needed. Our accident recovery team is trained to handle complex and urgent cases with care and professionalism.

Which Car Recovery Service Do You Need?

If your car suddenly won’t start or breaks down on the road, you likely need breakdown recovery. If your vehicle is damaged in a crash or collision, then accident recovery is essential for safety and legal compliance.
