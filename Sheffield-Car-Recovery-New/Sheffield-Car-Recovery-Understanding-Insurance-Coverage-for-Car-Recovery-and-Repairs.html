2. Repair Coverage and What’s Included in Your Policy

Policies vary on repair coverage, so it’s important to know if repairs are included after an incident. Comprehensive policies often provide recovery and repairs, whereas third-party policies may cover only damages to other vehicles.

How to Check Repair Coverage:

4. Exclusions and Limitations in Your Policy

Be aware of exclusions and limitations in your policy, such as distance limits or specific incidents that are not covered. This can help you avoid unexpected out-of-pocket expenses in an emergency.

Common Exclusions:

  • Towing limits, particularly for distances that exceed the coverage limit.
  • Exclusions for breakdowns caused by wear and tear or negligence.
