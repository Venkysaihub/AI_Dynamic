The Importance of Protecting the EV Battery During Recovery

The high-voltage lithium-ion battery in an EV is not only sensitive but also expensive. If damaged during recovery, it can lead to costly repairs or even safety hazards.

  • Risks of Battery Damage: Improper towing could damage the battery, making the vehicle unsafe or requiring costly repairs.
  • Fire Hazards: A damaged battery can result in thermal runaway, potentially causing dangerous fires.

Flatbed Towing: The Best Option for EV Recovery

Unlike traditional vehicles, EVs should never be towed with wheels on the ground, as this could damage the drivetrain. Flatbed towing ensures that the vehicle is completely lifted, thus protecting its sensitive components.

  • Protects the Battery and Motor: Flatbed towing helps avoid strain on the wheels, drivetrain, and battery, ensuring safe recovery.
  • Prevents Further Damage: Flatbed towing keeps the EV neutral, protecting its battery and motor from further damage.
