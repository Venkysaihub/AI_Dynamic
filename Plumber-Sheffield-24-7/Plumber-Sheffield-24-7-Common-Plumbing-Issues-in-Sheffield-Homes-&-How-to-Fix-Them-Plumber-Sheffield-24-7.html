1. Leaky Faucets

A dripping faucet is not only annoying but can also waste a significant amount of water over time. The most common cause is a worn-out washer, which can be easily replaced. If you’re unsure how to do this, reach out to Plumber Sheffield 24/7 for expert help.

2. Clogged Drains

3. Running Toilets

A running toilet can waste hundreds of gallons of water daily. This issue is usually due to a faulty flapper valve or an imbalanced float. Replacing these parts is relatively simple, but if you’re not confident, Plumber Sheffield 24/7 can handle it for you.

4. Low Water Pressure

Low water pressure can make simple tasks like showering or washing dishes frustrating. This problem is often caused by mineral buildup in the pipes or a hidden leak. A professional from Plumber Sheffield 24/7 can diagnose and resolve the issue quickly.

5. Burst Pipes

6. Water Heater Issues
