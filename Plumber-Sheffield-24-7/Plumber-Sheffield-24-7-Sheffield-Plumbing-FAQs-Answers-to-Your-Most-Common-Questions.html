1. When Should I Call an Emergency Plumber?

Some plumbing problems require immediate attention to prevent damage to your home. You should call an emergency plumber if you experience:

  • Burst Pipes: A burst pipe can cause extensive water damage if not fixed immediately.
  • Severe Leaks: Water leaks that cannot be stopped need urgent professional attention.
  • No Hot Water: If your boiler has stopped working, an emergency plumber can help restore hot water.
  • Blocked Drains: Severe blockages can cause sewage backups and flooding.

2. How Can I Prevent Blocked Drains?

Blocked drains are a common issue, but you can prevent them by following these tips:

  • Avoid pouring grease, oil, or fat down the sink.
  • Use drain strainers to catch food particles and debris.
  • Flush drains with hot water and baking soda regularly.
  • Dispose of wipes and hygiene products properly instead of flushing them.

3. What Are the Signs of a Hidden Water Leak?

Hidden leaks can cause structural damage and high water bills. Watch for these warning signs:

  • Unexplained increase in your water bill.
  • Damp spots or mold on walls and ceilings.
  • Low water pressure throughout your home.
  • Sounds of running water when no taps are on.

4. How Often Should My Boiler Be Serviced?

Your boiler should be serviced at least once a year to ensure it operates safely and efficiently. Regular servicing helps:

  • Prevent breakdowns and expensive repairs.
  • Improve energy efficiency and lower heating costs.
  • Ensure safety by detecting carbon monoxide leaks.
  • Maintain your boiler’s warranty requirements.

5. What Should I Do If My Radiators Aren’t Heating Properly?

If your radiators are not heating evenly, try the following:

  • Bleed the Radiators: Release trapped air to restore proper heating.
  • Check the Thermostat: Ensure it's set correctly and working properly.
  • Inspect the Boiler Pressure: Low pressure can affect heating efficiency.
  • Call a Plumber: If the issue persists, a professional may need to check for blockages or system issues.
