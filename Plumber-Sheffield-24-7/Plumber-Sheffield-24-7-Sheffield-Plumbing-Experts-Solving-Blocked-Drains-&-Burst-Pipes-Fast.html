What Is Plumbing Insurance Coverage?

Plumbing insurance coverage is a part of your home insurance policy that may cover the cost of emergency plumbing repairs, pipe replacements, and even boiler breakdowns. Knowing what’s included can save you time and money in an emergency.

1. Check Your Home Insurance Policy for Plumbing Cover

Many home insurance policies include coverage for plumbing emergencies, but the details vary. Understanding these terms helps ensure you're prepared.

How to Check for Plumbing Coverage:

  • Look for terms like “plumbing emergencies,” “boiler cover,” or “leak protection.”
  • Check for exclusions, such as wear and tear or lack of maintenance.
