1. Identifying Blocked Drains

Blocked drains occur when debris, grease, or foreign objects clog your pipes, leading to slow drainage or complete blockages.

How to Recognize Blocked Drains:

  • Water draining slowly in sinks, tubs, or toilets.
  • Foul odors emanating from drains.
