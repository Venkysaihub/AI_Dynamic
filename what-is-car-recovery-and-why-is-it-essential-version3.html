What Is Car Recovery?

Car recovery is the service that ensures vehicles that cannot be driven due to issues like breakdowns, accidents, or mechanical failures are transported to a safe location. This service typically includes:

  • Vehicle Breakdown Assistance: Recovery for mechanical issues that leave your car immobile.
  • Accident Recovery: After a collision, your vehicle needs careful handling to avoid further damage.
  • Flat Tire or Dead Battery Solutions: If roadside assistance isn't enough, recovery services are essential.
  • Legal Repossession: When vehicles are recovered for legal or financial reasons.
