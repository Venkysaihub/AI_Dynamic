What Is Car Recovery Insurance Coverage?

Car recovery coverage is a part of your insurance policy that covers the cost of towing or transporting your car after a breakdown or accident. This coverage can include roadside assistance, towing to a garage, and sometimes even repairs.

1. Check Your Insurance Policy for Roadside Assistance

Many insurance policies offer roadside assistance, which can cover towing, flat tires, fuel shortages, or other breakdown-related issues.

How to Check for Roadside Assistance:

  • Look for terms like “towing,” “roadside assistance,” or “emergency recovery.”
  • Understand towing limits and distances specified in your policy.
