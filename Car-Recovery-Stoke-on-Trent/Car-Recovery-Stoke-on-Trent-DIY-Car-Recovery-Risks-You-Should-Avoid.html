What Is DIY Car Recovery?

DIY car recovery involves taking matters into your own hands when your car breaks down or gets stuck. This can include using a tow rope, trying to move the car manually, or attempting minor repairs without professional assistance. While you might think it’s a quick fix, it can cause more harm than good.
